Frequenty Asked Questions

Below are the answers to some common questions.

Don't see the question you are looking for? Feel free to email your question to Carol Landry at clandry999@att.net

When may I visit the property?

You may coordinate with Unity's event host and arrange an appointment to come and take a look at this charming space.

Will you provide an officiant for the ceremony?

You may use an officiant of your choosing or Unity's minister is available to officiate. Reverend Carol Landry is the minister at Unity Church on the mountain. She has officiated weddings for many years.

How do I pay?

Currently Unity can only accept cash or check. At the time the venue is reserved a 50% deposit will be required. The remaining balance will be due 14 days prior to the day of your ceremony.

What is required to reserve a date for my event?

50% of the rental rate (as a non-refundable deposit) and a signed contract will reserve your date.

Do you have a reception space available?

Sorry, no available space for receptions. Unity Church venue is for wedding ceremonies only.
