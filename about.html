About Us

Unity Church on the Mountain is friendly, warm and welcoming and has been a part of the Huntsville area since the early 1970s, serving not only its members, but the community as well.

We are pleased and very excited to offer our charming and simple sanctuary as a ceremony venue for the couple planning a small, intimate gathering.

The sanctuary is soft, ethereal, and light filled, perfect for a day or evening wedding. The church itself is situated in a spot that is secluded and private, surrounded by trees you immediately feel relaxed and serene.

Non-denominational, interfaith unions and commitment ceremonies as well as nontraditional ceremonies are always welcome.
